# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- Works with Developers to track delivery progress and surface blockers early
- Aligns with Product Managers on scope, priorities, and trade-offs
- Collaborates with Change Managers, Business Analysts, QA Leads, and other roles to coordinate timelines and cross-functional dependencies

---

## Change Manager

### Role Summary
Oversees all changes to project scope, schedule, or deliverables. Serves as the central coordinator for evaluating and communicating the impact of changes across the project.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain and manage the change request log
- Assess impact of proposed changes on scope, schedule, budget, and quality
- Coordinate with Project Managers, Product Managers, and stakeholders to evaluate trade-offs
- Facilitate change approval workflows and communicate decisions to the team
- Ensure documentation is updated to reflect approved changes

### Goals
- Prevent uncontrolled scope creep
- Ensure all changes are evaluated, approved, and communicated systematically
- Maintain project stability and predictability

### Typical Communication
- Change request reviews with Project Managers and stakeholders
- Impact assessment reports shared with Product Managers
- Change log updates distributed to Developers and leads

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- Works closely with the Project Manager to track change impact on plans and timelines
- Collaborates with the Product Manager to evaluate business trade-offs
- Communicates change decisions to Developers so implementation is clearly understood

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Bridges the gap between business stakeholders and the technical team. Clarifies ambiguous requirements and ensures deliverables align with user needs and business goals.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit, document, and validate business and functional requirements
- Translate stakeholder needs into actionable user stories and acceptance criteria
- Facilitate requirements workshops and stakeholder interviews
- Review deliverables for alignment with documented requirements
- Support User Acceptance Testing (UAT) by defining test scenarios

### Goals
- Eliminate ambiguity in requirements before development begins
- Ensure deliverables meet actual business needs
- Reduce rework by catching misalignments early

### Typical Communication
- Requirements walkthroughs with Developers and Product Managers
- Stakeholder interviews and workshop facilitation
- Functional specification documents and user story refinements

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- Partners with the Product Manager to translate the product vision into detailed user stories
- Works directly with Developers to answer requirement questions during implementation
- Supports the Project Manager by surfacing requirement risks that may affect scope or timeline

---

## Quality Assurance Lead

### Role Summary
Defines and oversees test processes to ensure that deliverables meet quality standards and acceptance criteria before release.

### Responsibilities
- Define test strategy, test plans, and quality standards for the project
- Coordinate test execution across unit, integration, and acceptance testing phases
- Track and triage defects, ensuring resolution before release
- Collaborate with Developers on testability and coverage requirements
- Gate release readiness based on quality metrics

### Goals
- Prevent defects from reaching production
- Maintain high quality and reliability across all deliverables
- Provide clear quality metrics and traceability to acceptance criteria

### Typical Communication
- Test status reports and defect dashboards shared with Project Managers
- Defect triage sessions with Developers
- Release readiness sign-off communicated to the full team

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- Works closely with Developers to define test coverage expectations and resolve defects
- Partners with the Product Manager to verify acceptance criteria are testable
- Coordinates with the Project Manager to communicate test timelines and release readiness

---

## Communications Coordinator

### Role Summary
Acts as the central point for project communications, ensuring consistent, timely, and accurate information flows to internal and external stakeholders.

### Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain the project communications plan
- Draft and distribute project status updates, announcements, and reports
- Manage stakeholder communication calendars and distribution lists
- Ensure consistent messaging across all project touchpoints
- Coordinate with Project Managers on escalation communications

### Goals
- Maintain stakeholder trust through transparent and timely communication
- Prevent miscommunication and information silos
- Support informed decision-making through clear reporting

### Typical Communication
- Regular stakeholder newsletters and status summaries
- Coordination with Project Managers on milestone and risk communications
- Escalation notices and executive briefings as needed

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- Partners with the Project Manager to align on communication cadence and content
- Works with the Product Manager to communicate product decisions and roadmap updates
- Supports Developers by distributing relevant project context that may affect their work

---

## Support/Operations Liaison

### Role Summary
Provides input on production readiness and helps facilitate the smooth transition of project deliverables from deployment into day-to-day operations and support.

### Responsibilities
- Represent operational requirements and constraints during project planning
- Review deliverables for operational readiness (monitoring, runbooks, SLAs)
- Coordinate handoff from the project team to operations/support teams
- Identify post-release risks and operational support requirements
- Provide feedback from production into future project iterations

### Goals
- Ensure deliverables are production-ready before release
- Minimize operational disruption during and after deployment
- Create a continuous feedback loop between operations and project teams

### Typical Communication
- Production readiness reviews with Project Managers and Developers
- Handoff documentation shared with support and operations teams
- Post-release incident feedback relayed to the project team

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- Collaborates with Developers to ensure operational concerns (logging, monitoring, rollback) are addressed
- Works with the Project Manager to plan production deployment activities
- Partners with the Product Manager to surface operational constraints that may influence product decisions
