# OctoAcme Project Management Docs

## Overview

These documents define the project management processes used at OctoAcme, enabling centralized knowledge sharing and standardized execution. They cover the full project lifecycle from initiation through release and continuous improvement. This helps ensure repeatable project success, clearer roles and responsibilities, and improved onboarding for new team members.

### Process Areas

**Project Management Overview**
Provides a high-level introduction to how OctoAcme runs projects, including guiding principles, core roles, key artifacts, and the overall project lifecycle. It serves as the entry point for new team members to quickly understand the OctoAcme delivery approach.

**Project Initiation**
Covers the steps needed to validate and authorize new work — from confirming the business need and defining success metrics to aligning stakeholders and deciding whether to move forward. It ensures every project starts with a clear purpose and the right approvals.

**Project Planning**
Turns an approved initiative into an actionable backlog and delivery plan. It covers how to break work into shippable increments, define the Definition of Done, identify dependencies, and set release milestones so the team can execute with clarity.

**Execution & Tracking**
Guides day-to-day delivery practices, including team rhythms, pull request workflows, quality standards, and progress reporting. It helps teams stay aligned, surface blockers early, and maintain a consistent pace toward project goals.

**Risks & Communication**
Explains how to identify, assess, and mitigate risks throughout the project lifecycle, and how to keep stakeholders informed through structured communication templates and escalation paths. Proactive risk management reduces surprises and maintains stakeholder confidence.

**Release & Deployment**
Standardizes how OctoAcme releases features to production, covering release types, pre-release requirements, deployment checklists, and rollback procedures. A consistent release process reduces risk and makes deployments more predictable and observable.

**Retrospective & Continuous Improvement**
Provides a framework for capturing learnings after each sprint, release, or milestone and converting them into concrete action items. Regular retrospectives foster a culture of continuous improvement and help teams grow over time.

**Roles & Personas**
Defines the typical roles involved in OctoAcme projects — including Developers, Product Managers, and Project Managers — with their responsibilities, goals, and communication patterns. Clear role definitions reduce ambiguity and improve cross-functional collaboration.
